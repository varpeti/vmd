Inline math

Many symbols can also be displayed using a TeX alternative, `@ pm sqrt( sum(i=-oo; oo; Delta i) )*sqrt(5)` but a preceeding backslash is not required.

There is another example: `lim_{x->oo} lim_{y->oo} (1/(1/x + 1/y))` not math: (pi/2), math: `lim_{x->oo} lim_{y->oo} (1/(1/x + 1/y))` again, not math.
